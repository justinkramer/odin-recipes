Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and cheese is a staple in any American household.

Its creamy, cheesy delicious goodness is a comfort food in almost any household, and is a favorite for family members of all ages

Ingredients

  • 1 box of macaroni
  • 1 cup of cheese
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of butter

Directions

  1. Boil water
  2. Add macaroni
  3. Drain water
  4. Add cheese
  5. Enjoy!
