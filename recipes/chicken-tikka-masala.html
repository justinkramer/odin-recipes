Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken Tikka Masala is a delicious dish consisting of a savory sauce with a touch of sweetness and a hint of Indian spices.

It was created in the United States at a restaurant to cater to a Western clientelle and quickly became a staple at any Indian restaurant in America.

Ingredients

  • chicken, cooked to perfection in a Tandoori oven
  • tomato sauce
  • cream
  • onions
  • rice
  • naan
  • vegetable oil
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • garam masala
  • coriander
  • cumin
  • tumeric
  • paprika
  • fenugreek
  • cinnamon
  • cardamom
  • cayenne pepper
  • sugar
  • lemon juice
  • tomato paste
  • water
  • salt
  • pepper

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Saute garlic and ginger for 1 minute. Season with garam masala, coriander, cumin, turmeric, paprika, fenugreek, cinnamon, and cardamom. Stir in tomato sauce and cook for 2 minutes, stirring frequently. Stir in cream, sugar, and lemon juice. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Place chicken in sauce and simmer for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Serve with rice and naan.
