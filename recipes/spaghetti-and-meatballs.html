Spaghetti and Meatballs

Description

Spaghetti and meatballs is a staple in any Italian household.

Its delicious, meaty, saucy goodness is a comfort food in almost any household, and is a favorite for family members of all ages

Ingredients

  • 1 box of spaghetti
  • 1 cup of meatballs
  • 1/2 cup of sauce
  • 1/4 cup of butter

Directions

  1. Boil water
  2. Add spaghetti
  3. Drain water
  4. Add meatballs
  5. Add sauce
  6. Enjoy!
